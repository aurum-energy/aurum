About our partners.

Learn more about our close partners.

At Aurum Energy Inc. we believe that we have a duty to support our local community. This is why we are always on the look out to sponsor organizations and initiatives which share our values of team work, courage and excellence. If you are looking to get sponsored, we would love to hear from you.

Additionally, we work hand-in-hand with industry leaders. We have a close relationship with Coil and Pfister to ensure we are effectively contributing to a sustainable future, as well as with organizations such as SAASCAR.
